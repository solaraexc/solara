## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- ❌ **Executor won't inject on Windows**
  The Roblox client may have updated ahead of the executor. Check that you're running the latest version of **Solara Executor Windows** — open the app and look for the update prompt on the main screen.
  **Solution:** Update to the latest build, restart both Roblox and the executor, and try injecting again.

- ❌ **APK installation blocked on Android**
  Android's default security settings prevent installing apps from outside the Play Store.
  **Solution:** Go to `Settings > Security > Install Unknown Apps` and enable the permission for your file manager or browser. Then re-run the **Solara Executor APK** installer.

- ❌ **Scripts not executing after injection**
  This usually happens when the injection fires before the game has fully loaded its environment.
  **Solution:** Wait until you're fully loaded into the Roblox experience before pressing **Execute**. Alternatively, increase `inject_delay_ms` in `config.json` to `2500`.

- ❌ **iOS app crashes on launch**
  Expired sideloading certificates are the most common cause on iOS.
  **Solution:** Re-sign the **Solara Executor iOS IPA** through AltStore or Sideloadly and trust the new certificate under `Settings > General > Device Management`.

- ❌ **High CPU usage during execution**
  Some scripts with infinite loops or rapid polling intervals can spike processor usage.
  **Solution:** Review the script for unthrottled `while true` loops and add `wait(0.1)` delays. The built-in `script_timeout` setting will also kill runaway scripts after the configured limit.

- ❌ **Overlay not appearing on Android**
  The floating overlay requires explicit display-over-other-apps permission on Android 12 and above.
  **Solution:** Navigate to `Settings > Apps > Solara Executor > Display Over Other Apps` and toggle it on.
